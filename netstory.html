For this project, I wanted to go for a never-ending nightmare, almost similar to a maze.

You want to escape but every decisions needs like endless loop. Was there ever an option to leave in the first place?

There are two "final endings" to end the loop but there is only one pathway there. In addition, I wanted the music

to repeat on every page to create an entrapping feeling of where everything feels the same. There are some special music for special events.
